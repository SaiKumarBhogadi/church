Our Location

At JCAM Church, we are more than just a place of worship; we are a family united in faith and love. Rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ, we strive to create a welcoming and spiritually uplifting community where everyone, regardless of background, can experience God’s love, grace, and guidance. We believe in the power of prayer, worship, and fellowship, and we invite you to be part of this faith-filled journey. Whether you are looking for a place to grow spiritually, connect with fellow believers, or deepen your relationship with Christ, JCAM Church is here for you.
